11 APRIL 2019 Tiffany & Co. released a new engagement ring Tiffany True

A novelty that will become a classic

Cult of Tiffany & Co. most often associated with the classics of wedding jewelry traditions, therefore, the appearance of a new engagement ring, designed to become a new symbol of the brand, has become a significant event. Tiffany True is made in two versions: from platinum with a colorless diamond and from yellow gold with a fancy yellow diamond cut "cushion". For the new model, the masters developed a special faceting: the upper edge of the stone has a wide surface, which makes it possible to enhance its flickering.

Tiffany True is distinguished by several points. One of them is a frame in the form of an abstract "T", which frames a diamond. Instead of the traditional six valves, she has only four. In addition, it should be noted "landing" stone - it is located much lower in comparison with Tiffany Setting and looks more elegant. In general, the ring looks more compact, graphical and modern interpretation of the classic Tiffany Setting models, says Reed Krakoff.

Tiffany True will go on sale in April - just in time for the start of the wedding season.

110 APRIL 2019 Life after Calvin Klein: designer Rough Simons opens a cafe

What did one of the most talented fashion-creators of our time decide to do?

At the end of 2018, it was announced that Raf Simons was leaving the post of creative director Calvin Klein just two years after his appointment. What did the designer decide to do? The Belgian guru still has its own brand of men's clothing, under which he produces two collections a year and shows them in Paris. But is it really enough to realize all his indefatigable creative potential? Looks like no.

In a new interview with The Guardian, the designer said that he was waiting for him to come. It turned out that all his experience in fashion Simons decided to demonstrate in the furniture industry. And already cooperates with the Danish textile company Kvadrat. To present your interior works (including a snowstorm) at the prestigious Milan exhibition Salone del Mobile design, the designer decided to open a cafe.

Top 5 palettes of shadows for 2018.

5. PRISME BLACK SPARKLE, GIVENCHY

Our review opens with a tool that can provide a very long mattifying effect, and it also makes it possible to even out the skin tone almost perfectly. In the composition there are active components of acacia and red clay, which allows you to successfully hide even serious defects of the face and give it a natural and beautiful tone. The texture is quite thick, but the cream itself is airy and velvety. Means perfectly keeps on skin, is suitable also for fat integuments, but in the latter case, a slight shine may occur after a few hours. For this reason, this tool is well suited for normal and dry skin.

With the help of this cream it is possible to disguise even serious redness and get rid of minor defects, matte finish, looks very attractive. The cream does not fit skin with large or dilated pores - it will fall into them and make it very noticeable. It is applied easily, absorbed within a couple of minutes. The smell does not stand out, is characterized by a weak powdery aroma.

4. LES BEIGES HEALTHY GLOW NATURAL EYESHADOW PALETTE, CHANEL

This foundation incorporates the latest moisturizing formula. In addition, it has excellent sun protection characteristics, which allows to significantly reduce the negative environmental exposure. After applying the face there is a feeling of comfort. The structure is not particularly dense, it can be provided as a dense, and average coverage. First of all, this tool is designed for normal or dry skin. The cream is available in 19 shades, so you can choose the perfect for yourself option.

It is well resistant to low temperatures, so it can be used in the cold season: the cream will not roll, does not provoke an increase in fat content, still moisturizes well. Makeup artists recommend after applying the cream to add some amount of powder, since an excessively high content of moisturizing ingredients can cause some shine. On the face is distributed almost instantly, expense economical.

This is one of the most lasting remedies among all that have been included in our review of the best tone creams of 2018. You can not even use the powder.

During the day, the feeling of comfort remains, the skin retains its original velvety, there is no feeling of heaviness or tightness. Persistence is good, but is removed surprisingly simply with the help of any makeup remover.

2. VELVET TRIO SHADE RED, MAKE UP STORE

This is one of the newest makeup products released by a famous American company. In this tool there are adaptive particles that take on the natural color of the skin, making the cream itself completely invisible on its surface. Efficiency and versatility is one of the highest. To the touch it is light and has a slightly velvety surface, which is a bit like powder. The cream does not clog pores and gives the skin healthy shine, well hides small flaws, small pimples, patches of reddened skin. The composition has the so-called elastomers, allowing the skin to be velvety and healthy. The tool is perfect for sensitive skin, does not cause allergic reactions.

1. LOVE YOU SO MOCHI, NYX

This is the latest development of French cosmetologists. Shades of this cream is quite enough, due to which this product is suitable for girls with any skin color.

The texture of the cream is light and gentle, it will be evenly distributed over the face, does not create the sensation of a mask, also not provoked by the increased secretion of sebum. You can apply in different ways - with your fingers, with a brush or with a special sponge. This cream will be an excellent basis even for a rather complex makeup. It is quite acceptable to use this tool separately, but you can also slightly powder.

How to choose the right foundation?

We decided to start our review of the best creams to even out the skin color with the rules of their selection. Skin type strongly influences this factor. If a woman has oily or mixed skin type, then you should get a cream containing zinc, sulfur, vitamins belonging to groups A and B. With their help, you can get rid of unpleasant shine and reduce sebum. If the cream has a dense structure, then in this case its use should be abandoned. To correct certain areas of the face, use a special matting pencil.

For girls with dry skin, moisturizing concealers that are allowed to use all year round will be ideal. They can be found hyaluronic, aloe extract and a number of other components, with which moisture will be retained in the skin. In addition, they have oils, proteins and a complex of vitamins that make the skin elastic and very soft. One of the best options for dry skin is BB-cream - one of the newest developments by cosmetologists. It provides good skin care, making it smooth and ultraviolet resistant. It is produced on a water-gel base, due to which it will ideally fall on the surface of the skin.

Mature skin will feel great under a layer of cream with a lifting effect. Such compounds easily mask small wrinkles, remove uneven places and quickly cope with all sorts of inflammation. They additionally moisturize the skin and make the relief of the face more even, as well as protect it from all sorts of negative external influences.

It is also necessary to correctly select the toner in color - professional makeup artists say that it should strictly match the color of the skin. The cream should give the face a natural and healthy shade. Before buying funds should be mandatory to test the device on the skin.

In developing our ranking of the best tonal creams, we took into account all the factors listed above, took into account the ratio of price and quality, and also reviews of users and professional cosmetologists.

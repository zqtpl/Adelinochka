Hello!

Today we will talk about the recent brand Darphin, about the restorative concentrate with retinol for the face in capsules for evening use. This product was released as part of the anti-aging line of the brand - Ideal Resource, and is intended to moisturize, nourish the skin, improve its tone, make it shine, and also reduce facial and age wrinkles.

The tool is an oil concentrate with retinol, the percentage of which is 0.3%. It may seem to many that it is not enough, but it is not. It is known that the encapsulated retinol is stable and works in such a percentage much more efficiently than, for example, a product with almost 2% of a free substance in its usual form. In addition to retinol capsules contain various oils. The main ones are: jojoba oil, macadamia, sesame, argan, which moisturize well, nourish the skin, give it a healthy appearance, and also help soften the harsh effects of retinol.

For the first time I used a remedy with the content of retinol capsules, and I should note that it is very convenient. First and foremost, it is the complete preservation of the substance until it is applied to your skin. Secondly, it is not necessary to think too much about the amount of money applied to the face; the manufacturer has already done this for you. Thirdly, you have a full course, and personally it disciplines me a lot. And also, I really liked the fact that oil concentrate, because for women with age or dry skin, this is a great opportunity, without fear of consequences, to use the product with retinol.
